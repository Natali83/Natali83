Problem statement

Proposal

Explore new ways to display information to create a more fun experience.

Understanding the context

The company is a service platform for fashion brands and retailers that uses artificial intelligence to predict customer demand.

They enable fashion companies to gain insights into their customers' needs, allowing them to produce efficiently while maintaining profitability and sustainability.

“We want to enable brands to produce only what consumers want by predicting demand.”

What we found

Surveys

The surveys were mostly completed through Facebook.

Pain points

  • On some devices, the typography overlaps the buttons.
  • The font size is too small and the text is difficult to read as a result.
  • The layout does not take advantage of the full screen.
  • The use of color is focused on continuing but not on the main task.
  • The navigation bar takes up too much space for a single icon.
  • The composition of the elements corresponds to an old and boring form.
  • The aesthetic in general don't propone nothing exciting.

Opportunities

Structure analysis

The idea was to replace the way people choose their option.

The "next" button was taking up valuable space, so I decided not to give it that much prominence. Instead of making it so relevant, I could use another way, like the "On-boarding" style.
