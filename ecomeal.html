Why and when do people cook at home?

Research has shown that people are cooking more. Healthy food and cheapness have been the biggest factors leading to the increase. However, various lockdown measures during the pandemic have been a further contributing factor to the rise.
Even though they are a little tired after work, most interviewees choose to cook dinner, as this is when their time and attention can be focussed in the kitchen.

Why do people prefer Mil kit Service?

Cooking at home is definitely considered healthier, but at eve so it takes a bit of time. Time is by far the most important consideration for most of those who work all day.
One of the main reasons delivery services have increased is the opportunity to eat a variety of healthy foods and save time.

Who is our user?

Millennials in the upper-middle income bracket between the ages of 32 and 45 who live in large cities.
The user wants to spend less time planning meals because they work full time and then get tired of thinking about what to eat.
It is also important for them to have a variety of foods and to eat healthily.
