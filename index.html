Natali Salazar Based in Argentina (GMT -3)

Beginning

I graduated as Graphic Designer at University of Buenos Aires (UBA) in 2008. Since then I have been working for many companies related to technology, marketing, publicity and design.

Curiosity

I have always been trying to get more resources and tools to improve my work. In this search, I studied a post-grade course named Logical and technique of form (UBA) in 2010. This course was multidisciplinary so I studied with people from other fields and different backgrounds. For me, it was enriching to analyze the same thing in other perspectives and points of view. I realized that getting different views of the same thing opens your eyes to gain a deep understanding of the problem.

Turning point

After many years of taking courses about mobile, usability, user interfaces, programming I started to feel that I needed a change. In 2015, I had a strong necessity to know more about User Experience so I decided to find out more about it. I read about Behavioral Psychology, Human centered design, Design Thinking, Artificial Intelligence, everything that could help me to open my mind and acquire knowledge to create better products or services that facilitate the life of my community.

In 2016, I came across Interaction Design Foundation. Since then, I have been taking different courses and I started applying the Design Thinking methodology to create better solutions.

Next

The pandemic has brought to the surface a huge number of social problems and necessities. As a designer I can't turn a blind eye. I believe it's time for us to get involved and engaged with our reality.
